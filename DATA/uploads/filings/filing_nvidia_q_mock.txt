NVIDIA reported strong demand for data center GPUs, with growth supported by large enterprise and hyperscaler AI spending.
Management stated that near-term revenue visibility remains strong, but supply constraints and customer concentration remain notable concerns.
The company expects continued capital intensity across the AI ecosystem and acknowledged that valuation expectations are elevated.
Risk factors include semiconductor supply disruption, export restrictions, competition from alternative accelerators, and cyclical volatility in technology spending.
Management also noted that any delay in customer deployment schedules could affect short-term revenue timing.
