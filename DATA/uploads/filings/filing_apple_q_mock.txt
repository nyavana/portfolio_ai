Apple Inc. reported steady quarterly revenue growth driven by services and resilient iPhone demand.
Management highlighted continued investment in artificial intelligence features, custom silicon, and cloud infrastructure.
The company noted that gross margin remained stable, but future margins could face pressure from higher capital expenditures, supply chain adjustments, and foreign exchange fluctuations.
Management also warned that macroeconomic uncertainty in major international markets could reduce consumer spending.
Key risks discussed include margin compression, regulatory scrutiny, dependence on hardware upgrade cycles, and execution risk in AI product rollout.
